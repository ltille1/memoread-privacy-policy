Privacy Policy – MemoRead Journal

MemoRead Journal respects your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

1. Email Address

Your email address is collected through Firebase Authentication to:

  • Create your user account
  • Log you into the app on your devices
  • Secure access to your stored reading data

2. User Content (Stored in Firebase Firestore)

The app stores the following user-generated content:

  • Book references
  • Reading notes
  • Tags, categories, shelves or other custom metadata

These items are stored securely in Firebase Firestore and linked to your account in order to synchronize your data across devices.

How Your Data Is Used

  • To provide the core functionality of the app
  • To save and sync your reading notes and book entries
  • To ensure your data is available across your devices

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your data
  • We do not use your data for advertising
  • We do not engage in cross-app tracking
  • We do not share your data with third parties

Third-Party Services

MemoRead Journal uses Google Firebase services. For more information on how Firebase handles data, please refer to the Firebase Privacy Notice:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Data Retention

Your reading data is stored for as long as your account exists. You may request deletion at any time.
